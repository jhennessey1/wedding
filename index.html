Alli and Joey's Wedding Countdown

Alli and Joey both grew up in beautiful Conifer, Colorado (now where they are getting married!). They both went to Conifer High School, where they eventually met and fell in love!  After Alli's senior year of high school and Joey's freshman year at University of Northern Colorado, fate brought them together at the University of Colorado at Boulder where they both graduated with their Bachelor's. They have been together ever since, accompanying each other to Fort Collins after graduation and then eventually Denver where they settled into their careers. They have gone on many adventures together to Iceland, England and Colombia, and some closer to home. They have two wonderful dogs together and are very excited to share in their special day with you!
